Mock Interview Registration

 

 

 

To register for CLS Mock Interviews:

1. Log-in to your Tiger Recruiting Link (TRL) account or create a new TRL account: www.jobs.auburn.edu

2. Click on "Events" in the navy bar

3. Click on "Workshops" in the gray bar

4. Under the RSVP section, select a time slot (Times are offered in two rooms on two days. Please select only one slot.)

5. After selecting the time slot click "RSVP"
