Job Search Strategies for International Students
in the United States

 

The Auburn University Career Center is hosting a job search strategies series to help prepare international students for a job search within the United States. Students will learn about resume and cover letter writing, business etiquette, professional dress and interview strategies. Individual resume critiques are also available. Students may bring their lunches. Snacks and beverages are provided.

All sessions will be held in Room 2222, AU Student Center. The schedule is as follows:

Tuesday, March 20
• 12 PM | Resumes, Cover Letters, CV's Presentation

Wednesday, March 21
• 12 PM | Business Etiquette Presentation
• 1 PM | Resume Critique Available

Thursday, March 22
• 12 PM | Interview Presentation
• 1 PM | Resume Critique Available
