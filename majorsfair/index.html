Career Center > Major Exploration Day

Thursday October 17

Auburn University Student Center Ballroom
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

It is estimated that anywhere from 60-80% of college students will change their major at least once! Major exploration day brings you the opportunity to learn more about the many majors and minors that Auburn University has to offer and to explore career options with each major.

 

Free pizza will be provided

 

This event can help you learn about:
  • Majors offered by each college and school at Auburn University
  • Application requirements and deadlines (if required)
  • How to choose a major
  • Methods of exploring career options based on your major
