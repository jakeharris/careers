Donor and Supporter Information

Sponsorship Opportunities

In order to make this week long celebration of student employment a success, the Auburn University Career Center asks for your support through donations of prizes, and/or promotional items. National Student Employment Week (NSEW) presents a unique and rare opportunity for your business to have an on-campus marketing presence.

Please contact Jay Skipworth at 334-844-3864 or skipworth@auburn.edu if you are interested in a sponorship or donor opportunity.

You can be a sponsor in one of the following ways:
