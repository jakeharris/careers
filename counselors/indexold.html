Meet Your Career Counselors/Advisors

Career Counselors/Advisors are available to meet with students to provide assistance in all areas related to the career planning process including the following:

  • choosing a major
  • researching careers
  • career assessment
  • resumes and cover letter writing
  • job search techniques
  • interviewing skills

Availability:

Walk-in Hours: Counselors/Advisors are available to meet with students on a walk-in basis for a brief 15-20 minute session for assistance with resumes and cover letters, job search resources, and introduction to choosing/changing majors.

Walk-In Hours

  • Mon - Fri | noon - 4 pm | 303 Martin Hall

*To see when a Career Counselor/Advisor is available for walk-ins on location in the schools and colleges across campus, click on the links below.

Appointments - For more in depth career assistance in choosing or changing majors, job search, mock interviews, or assessment interpretations, it is recommended you make an appointment to see a Career Counselor. Appointment can be made by calling the Career Center at 334-844-4744.
