Career Center > Experience Week

Monday
February 4
Dress for Success Fashion Show
6-7 PM | The Hotel at AU and Dixon Conference Center
 

“Does this suit make my experience look big?” You’ll make a great first impression when interacting with employers at networking events and on job and internship interviews by dressing for success. Learn business professional fashion dos and don’ts from Dillard’s Style Expert, Amber Menzie with the help of the AU Modeling Board. Door prizes and free food round out this fun event.

Tuesday
February 5

How to Land an Internship
4-5 PM | AU Student Center Rm 2218
 

You get it. Internships are important. But, how do you go about finding and applying for internships that fit your career goals and interests? This session will cover the ins and outs of finding great internship opportunities with tips and strategies for submitting stand out applications and acing the interview process.

Wednesday
February 6

Guest Speaker & Internship Expert: Lauren Berger, The Intern Queen
6:30 - 8 PM | AU Student Center Rm 2218
 

You don’t get the nickname “Intern Queen” from sitting in your room watching re-runs of Law & Order: [Insert your choice of spin-off]. Internship Expert, Lauren Berger, participated in 15 internships during her 4 years of college including MTV, NBC, Fox and BWR Public Relations. She will share her experiences in finding internships, dealing with rejection and making valuable professional connections. Her website is dedicated to helping students find and apply for internships while also educating them on how to make the most of their experiences.

Thursday
February 7

Student Panel: Making the Most of Your Experience
4-5 PM | AU Student Center Rm 2218
 

There is more to most internships that making copies and getting coffee. To make a great impression on your employer and prepare yourself for a full-time job, you have to take advantage of every opportunity to learn and do more. Hear from students who have participated in internships as they discuss their experiences and answering your questions about the internship application process and how to make the most of your internship experience.

Monday
February 11

How to Market Your Experience
4-5 PM | AU Student Center Rm 2218
 

“New graduate with experience going once, going twice…sold!” An experience is only as good as your ability to market key skills and accomplishments to future employers. The session will teach you how to evaluate your experience, identify skills and accomplishments employers want to see and incorporate them into each aspect of the job search process (resume, cover letter, interview, etc.).

Tuesday
February 12

Career Expo: Engineering & Technical Majors
4-5 PM | AU Student Center Rm 2218
 

Renting a billboard on I-85 is the only other way to get yourself in front of this many employers at one time. Meet with representatives from over 100 companies and tell them why you are the best candidate for their internships and jobs. It’s a win-win. They want to hire Auburn graduates and you want to find employment. Check out a list of registered companies and get tips for preparing for expo here.
