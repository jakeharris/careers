Career Center > Experience > Spotlight - Livy

This is my experience...

Livy Hoskins

- Graphic Design intern with Auburn University's Division of Student Affairs since May 2012

- May 2013 graduate in Graphic Design

See More Spotlights! Intern Spotlight Archive
Tell us about yourself

I am a senior and currently completing my final semester at Auburn University. I have enjoyed my time at Auburn immensely, especially my course of study. Graphic Design has led to so many exciting opportunities! Before my current internship I was a Resident Assistant for two years, which was also a lot of fun. I’m very enthusiastic about my work, whatever it may be. I also have an optimistic attitude that helps me find the positive in every situation!

Where did you intern and how did you find this opportunity?

I am currently interning at Auburn University’s Division of Student Affairs as a Student Graphic Designer. I first started in this position in May 2012. Lady Cox, the Chief of Staff in the Division of Student Affairs, sent an email to one of my professors who in turn sent it to everyone in the graphic design program.

Describe your internship experience and the responsibilities you had.

My experience has been incredibly valuable in my development as a young professional. Not only have I been able to get real­world experience in the field of my choice, I have also gained experience working in an office environment. I work in collaboration with Chelsea Payne, our communications specialist, and another student designer. A few of my responsibilities include producing print material such as posters, brochures, invitations, and newsletters for any department in the Division of Student Affairs. I also keep the Student Affairs website updated and looking good!

Do you recommend that other Auburn students participate in internships and why or why not?

Absolutely. I believe that internships help give students a taste of the career they are considering. They also give students the chance to gain outside-the-classroom experience that they would not otherwise receive. I think this experience is very important to ensure future success in the career of their choice.

What are your career plans following graduation?

I intend to get a position as a junior designer in a small studio within the southeast. However, graphic design is one of those careers that can be done anywhere and everywhere so I’m not quite sure where I’ll finally end up!

What advice do you have for other Auburn students looking to gain experience while in college?

Get a job, if you have time. Even if it’s not something related to the field you expect to get into you can build some valuable skills just working with other people. And of course, participate in an internship!
