Departmental Contacts

Students considering credit bearing internships in the following majors should contact the faculty/staff person listed for the major to discuss course credit requirements. Please note that not all academic programs allow room in the curriculum for credit bearing internships. Students who are not able to receive credit through their academic program are encouraged to seek non-credit bearing internships during academic breaks.
