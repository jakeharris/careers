All the information in this sidebar is customizeable. You can even add orange line breakers with customizable widths.

Whenever you use the <p> tag for spacing, a padding of 6 pixels is added to the left and right side. This can be overridden with your own style attribute in the <p> tag.

You can also put pictures in the sidebar that are flush on the sides (width is 205 pixels) by using <div> for spacing instead of <p>.

Everything surrounding this test was included from other files by the browser.
