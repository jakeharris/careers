Services

Tiger Recruiting Link (TRL)

An online career management system serving as your access to Auburn students and recent graduates. Set up a free account to list full-time jobs and internship opportunities, schedule on-campus interviews, search and screen resumes, and register for career fairs.

  • Register for a New TRL Account (PDF)
On-Campus Recruiting (OCR)

OCR offers employers the opportunity to identify, screen and interview upcoming graduates for positions within their organizations. Meet face to face with applicants in a professional environment and screen for second round site interviews. Utilize 11 private, comfortably furnished rooms with wireless internet access.

  • On-Campus Recruiting Options
  • On-Campus Recruiting Guide (PDF)
Career Fairs and Special Events

Take part in one of approximately nine career fairs hosted each year and connect with Auburn students and graduates. The Career Center hosts the campus-wide Career Expo semi-annually, in February and September. Additional events include the Part-time Job Fair (August), Internship and Summer Job Fair (January) and Educational Interview Day (semi-annually in October and February). Register in TRL.

  • Special Events Calendar
Part-time Jobs and Student Employment

Advertise and post part-time and seasonal positions in TRL to help students obtain part-time employment while enrolled in college.

  • Student Employment
Information Sessions

Host an information session to inform students and faculty about careers with your organization. The Career Center can help you identify and reserve a location for your information session and promote the event to students through TRL.

  • Plan an Information Session (PDF)
Campus Visits

Plan a visit to Auburn and meet with faculty, staff, and student groups to discuss career opportunities with your organization. The Career Center can assist you in scheduling meetings and information sessions to increase your organization's exposure at Auburn.

  • Plan Your Visit
