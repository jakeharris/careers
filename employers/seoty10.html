Recognition for Student Employees

  1. Treat Day - full-time staff bring in treats/lunch for student workers.
  2. Treat Week – each day bring something different – cookies, popcorn, candy, etc.
  3. Take pictures - post student employee pictures and list what wonderful things they do
  4. Buy low cost, blank certificates and make up silly awards for students: best telephone voice, best web-surfer, etc.
  5. Good Egg Award - buy plastic Easter Eggs, fill with goodies and give out with “Good Egg” certificates. List what the student did to deserve this award.
  6. Cookies, Cookies, Cookies – Students love treats
  7. Snack Goodie Bag- Fill with small packs of crackers, cookies, candy and chips
  8. Popcorn – Fill a bowl or individual mini bowls daily, inexpensive yet tasty.
  9. Fruit – Have bowl of fresh fruit – take out- apples, plums, oranges etc.
  10. Pat on the Back - Draw an outline of your hand on a piece of paper. Photocopy enough copies for all students in your office. On each hand write a note that tells the student why they deserve a “Pat on the Back”.
  11. Use this week as an opportunity to start an award recognition program. Look around the office for a “white elephant” item. Name the item for the award (Employee of the Month/Week, Great Idea Award, Gotcha Award - catch someone in the act of doing something good) and then make the first presentation of it during Student Employment Week.
  12. Make a banner to hang for the whole week in your office.
    Decorate an office bulletin board or the office door for the week in recognition of your students.
  13. Use YOUR imagination…. A simple THANK YOU for all you do will go a long way!
