Planning Your Visit


Auburn University is located in Auburn, Alabama approximately 55 miles northeast of Montgomery, AL; 100 miles southwest of Atlanta Hartsfield International Airport; and 130 miles southeast of Birmingham, AL in the east-central part of the state.

PLEASE NOTE: Auburn, Alabama is located in the Central Time Zone. Please keep this in mind when making travel arrangements.

The Hotel at Auburn University

 

The Hotel at Auburn University and Dixon Conference Center is located one block from the Auburn University Career Center. If you are staying at the Hotel during your visit you can leave your car in the Hotel parking lot and walk onto campus.

 

Career Center Address:303 Martin Hall
 211 W. Thach Ave
 Auburn University, AL 36849

Directions to Auburn


To Auburn From Montgomery on North on I-85:
  • From I-85 N take EXIT 51
  • At the top of the ramp turn LEFT at the light onto S. College Street.
  • Continue on S. College Street for about 4 miles. You will see the Hotel at Auburn University on your right. Turn left at the next traffic light onto Thach Ave. Mary Martin Hall is at the end of the block on the left.
To Auburn From Atlanta South on I-85:
  • From I-85 S take Alabama EXIT 51
  • Turn RIGHT onto S. College Street.
  • Continue on S. College Street for about 4 miles. You will see the Hotel at Auburn University on your right. Turn left at the traffic light onto Thach Ave. Mary Martin Hall is at the end of the block on the left.

To Auburn from Birmingham on Highway US-280E:
  • Take a RIGHT off US-280E onto AL-147/College Street (there will be a Conoco Service Station at the intersection)
  • Stay on AL-147 for 4.8 miles. AL-147 will turn into College Street.
  • After you cross the railroad tracks, Thach Ave. will be the third traffic light. Turn right onto Thach Ave. Mary Martin Hall is at the end of the block on the left.
 

Parking for Martin Hall location:

As on most college campuses, parking is very limited. A Guest Parking Pass will be e-mailed to you upon request. Please contact Mr. Jay Skipworth at skipworth@auburn.edu, prior to the day of your visit to have a Guest Parking Pass e-mailed to you. If you do not obtain a parking pass via e-mail, you will need to pick up a Guest Parking Pass from the Parking Services Office, which is located at 330 Lem Morison Drive. For your convenience, a drive thru window is located on the ground level of the Parking Services Office and guest parking permits may be obtained there. The Guest Parking Pass allows visitors to park in zoned campus lots in marked parking spaces. The most convenient parking area for access to Mary Martin Hall and the Career Center is in the RBD Library parking deck located on the south side of the RBD Library, off of Roosevelt Drive. If you have a handicap placard or license plate, an Auburn University parking permit is still required to park on the campus.

Restaurants in Auburn


There are many fine restaurants in the downtown Auburn area that are convenient to Mary Martin Hall and Lowder Business Building. There are also several restaurants that will deliver your food. There is a notebook of restaurants and their menus available at the fourth floor desk (in Martin Hall).

For more information, visit The Auburn-Opelika Convention & Visitors Bureau.
