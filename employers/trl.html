Educational Support Services

 

Tiger Recruiting Link provides following services to employers:

ALL ON-CAMPUS RECRUITING (OCR) activities including requesting interview schedules, posting OCR jobs, viewing student resumes submitted to your job(s), inviting students to interview, viewing your On-Campus Recruiting schedule(s), and more.
SPECIAL EVENT REGISTRATION including Career Expo, Internship & Summer Job Fair, Education Interview Day ... just to name a few.
JOB POSTING for NON On-Campus Recruiting positions (those which won't need an interview schedule because interviews are not being held at AU.)
RESUME BOOKS: Access Auburn student and alumni resumes. (*Third-Party Recruiters are restricted from this area due to student privacy policies.)

(Note: all services offered by Auburn Career Services are free to employers except fees associated with attending Special Events)
