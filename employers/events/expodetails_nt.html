Non-Technical Career Expo Details

Non-Technical

    Additional Information

Which Day(s) Should I Attend?

Organizations are welcome to come to one or both Career Expo events as students of all majors will attend both Expo days. However, if you are targeting a few specific majors, you may choose to attend the day that is geared toward your industry.

Engineering & Technical: Monday, September 17, 2012 will feature employers recruiting for the following: engineering, computer science, construction, information technology, agriculture, scientific research, design, and additional fields with a technical or scientific focus.

Non-Technical: Wednesday, September 19, 2012 will feature employers recruiting for the following: business, accounting, banking, government, human services, retail, sales, communication, liberal arts, international relations, and additional fields without a technical or scientific focus.

Event Schedule

Please arrive on time and be ready to see students at the start of the event (3:00 PM). If not present by 3:30 PM, you will be considered a “NO SHOW”. Your space can then be assigned to another organization.

12:00 PM-3:00 PM Employer Set-up & Registration
2:00 PM-8:00 PM Hospitality Room Open (representatives only)
3:00 PM-8:00 PM Career Expo Open to Students
8:00 PM Expo Ends

Please plan to stay until 8:00 PM. The time has been publicized and students expect company representatives to be available up until that time.

Interviewing the Following Day

Non-Technical Career Expo
Thursday, September 20
8:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Procedure: You have the option to conduct interviews in Hotel and Conference Center the day following the Non-Technical Career Expo. If you are interested in Next Day Interviews, please select this option when completing the registration form in Tiger Recruiting Link.

Scheduling forms will be provided at Career Expo check-in. Employers will meet and schedule students the day of the Non-Technical Career Expo to be interviewed the following day, Thursday, September 20. ALL interviews will be conducted at The Hotel at Auburn University and Dixon Conference Center.
Lunch will be on your own.

Registration Fee

Registration Fee: $600 per table, per day. The total registration fee must be paid in full by Friday, August 17, 2012 to keep your reservation.

Registration fee includes:

  • 1 table (3' x 6')
  • 4 representatives
  • Lunch; dinner; snacks and beverages during the event
  • Parking
  • Electricity (please bring your own extension cords and power strips)
  • Company information printed in event flyer

Star Sponsorship: By contributing an additional $500, your organization can be a Star Sponsor. Final deadline to register as a “Star Sponsor” is Friday, August 17, 2012.

Star Sponsorship includes:

  • Your choice of booth location
  • Featured promotion in all event publicity
  • Company logo on Career Center website (through Fall 2011 semester)
