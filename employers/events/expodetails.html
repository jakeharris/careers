Career Expo Details

Engineering & Technical

    Additional Information

 

Which Day(s) Should I Attend?

Organizations are welcome to come to one or both Career Expo events as students of all majors will attend both Expo days. However, if you are targeting a few specific majors, you may choose to attend the day that is geared toward your industry.

Engineering & Technical: Monday September 19, 2011 will feature employers recruiting for engineering and technical careers.

Non-Technical: Wednesday September 21, 2011 will feature employers recruiting for non-technical careers.

Event Schedule

Please arrive on time and be ready to see students at the start of the event (3:00 PM). If not present by 3:30 PM, you will be considered a “NO SHOW”. Your space can then be assigned to another organization. Please review the No Show and Cancellation policies on the Expo Policies page.

12:00 PM-3:00 PM Employer Set-up & Registration
1:00 PM-7:00 PM Hospitality Room Open (representatives only)
3:00 PM-7:00 PM Career Expo Open to Students
7:00 PM Expo Ends

Please plan to stay until 7:00 PM. The time has been publicized and students expect company representatives to be available up until that time.

Next Day On-Site Interviews

Tuesday, September 20
8:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Prior to Expo: You have the option to conduct interviews in Hotel and Conference Center the day following the Engineering & Technical Career Expo. If you are interested in Next Day Interviews, please select this option when completing the registration form in Tiger Recruiting Link.

Day of Expo: Scheduling forms will be provided at check-in. Employers will meet and schedule students the day of the Engineering and Technical Career Expo to be interviewed the following day, Tuesday, September 20. ALL interviews will be conducted at The Hotel at Auburn University and Dixon Conference Center.
Lunch will be on your own.

Registration Fee

Registration Fee: $600 per table, per day. The total registration fee must be paid in full by Friday, August 19, 2011 to keep your reservation.

Registration fee includes:

  • 1 table (3' x 6'), 2 chairs
  • 4 representatives
  • Lunch; dinner; snacks and beverages during the event
  • Parking
  • Electricity (please bring your own extension cords and power strips)
  • Company information printed in event flyer

Star Sponsorship: By contributing an additional $500, your organization can be a Star Sponsor. Final deadline to register as a “Star Sponsor” is Friday, August 19, 2011.

Star Sponsorship includes:

  • Your choice of booth location
  • Featured promotion in event flyers and posters, and on Career Center website (through Fall 2011 semester)
