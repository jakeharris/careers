Graduate and Professional School Day

Wednesday, October 5, 2011
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
Auburn University - Student Center

Event Description

The Graduate and Professional School Fair is an event for students interested in graduate studies to learn more about opportunities at Auburn University. Graduate School and departmental representatives will be available with admission, program requirements, and funding information. The Graduate School will also provide information on becoming a more competitive applicant.
