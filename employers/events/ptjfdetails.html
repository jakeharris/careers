Part-Time Job Fair Details & Guidelines


Printable Tip Sheet


Part-Time Job Fair


 

Registration Fee

There is no fee ($0) to attend this event. We are glad to provide this program at no cost and appreciate your participation. You may register up to two (2) representatives to attend.

Registration includes:

  • 1 table (3' x 6'), 1 chair
  • 2 representatives
  • Parking
  • Electricity (please bring your own extension cords and power strips)
  • Company information printed in event flyer
Event Schedule

Please arrive on time and be ready to see students at the start of the event (10:00 AM). If not present by 10:30 AM, you will be considered a “NO SHOW”. Your space can then be assigned to another organization.

8:00 AM-10:00 AM Representative Set-up & Registration
10:00 AM-1:00 PM Part-Time Job Fair open to Students
11:30 AM-12:00 PM Representative Lunch (one box per rep)
1:00 PM Part-Time Job Fair Ends

Please plan to stay until 1:00 PM. The time has been publicized and students expect company representatives to be available up until that time.

Cancellation Policy

Please understand that we are reserving a space for your and should you have to cancel, please call the Auburn University Career Center as soon as possible at (334) 844-3862 and speak with Diane Thorne, Special Events Registration Manager. An e-mail must also be sent to Diane Thorne at thornde@auburn.edu explaining that you are officially canceling your organization's participation in the events. Diane will send you an e-mail confirming your cancellation. It is important to keep a copy of this communication as documentation for future reference. We appreciate your cooperation and support in our programs.

Space & Equipment

One 3 x 6 foot table and one chair will be provided per organization. Your display needs to fit within the space provided. Electricity will be available. Please bring your own extension cords and power strips.

    Display Guidelines

    Space is limited. No Additional Space Can Be Provided beyond one 3 x 6 foot table.

    Do not bring large displays, furniture, or equipment.

    Do not rearrange your table, occupy an adjacent vacant table or move to another location without approval from Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator. Staff will be available to help you relocate and move your materials, if needed.

    Exhibitor Guidelines
  • Decorations, signs and posters must be coordinated with the Auburn University Student Center (AUSC) staff prior to displaying in the Student Center. No tacks, nails, staples, tape or other fasteners are allowed. Exibitors are liable for any defacement of the facility as a result of their display!
  • The exhibitor is entirely responsible for the space leased by him/her and is responsible for keeping space free from any condiions which would be dangerous to persons coming upon the premises.
  • The management of the AUSC reserves the right to restrict the operation of any exhibit, which in its opinion detracts from the general character of the facility. This includes, but is not limited to, exhibits, which because of noice, flashing light, method of operation or display of unsuitable material are determined by management as objectionable.
  • The Auburn University Career Center and Student Center will not be responsible for any injury that may arise to exhibitors or their employees or for loss of or any damage to any good from any course whatsoever while in transit to or form or while in the AUSC.

We request your full cooperation in observing these guidelines. Anything not included in the rules and regulations will be subject to the discreion of Auburn University management.
