Graduate & Professional School Details


Print the Event Tip Sheet

 

Graduate & Professional

 

Event Schedule

Please arrive on time and be ready to see students at the start of the event (10:00 AM). If not present by 10:30 AM, you will be considered a “NO SHOW”. Your space can then be assigned to another organization.

8:00 AM-10:00 AM Representative Registration and Set-up
10:00 AM-1:00 PM Fair Open to Students
11:30 AM-12:00PM Representative Lunch (1 box per rep)
1:00 PM Fair Ends

Please plan to stay until 1:00 PM. The time has been publicized and students expect representatives to be available up until that time.

Registration Fee

Registration Fee: $400. The event takes place in 6 locations over 5 days. You have the option to attend one, some or all locations. This fee will be the same whether you attend one, some, or all locations.  No discounted fee is offered to representatives choosing to attend only selected locations. A separate fee will be charged for each individual graduate or professional school program.

Registration fee includes (at Auburn University site):

  • 1 table (3' x 6'), chair for each representative
  • 2 representatives (Additional $10 fee per rep over 2 for each location attending)
  • 1 boxed lunch per representative
  • Parking
  • Electricity (please bring your own extension cords and power strips)
  • Organization information printed in event flyer

Please visit the Alabama Connection Website for complete Registration information.

    Space & Equipment

    One 3 x 6 foot table and one chair will be provided per organization. Your display needs to fit within the space provided. Electricity will be available. Please bring your own extension cords and power strips.

      Display Guidelines

      Space is limited. No Additional Space Can Be Provided beyond one 3 x 6 foot table.

      Do not bring large displays, furniture, or equipment.

      Do not rearrange your table, occupy an adjacent vacant table or move to another location without approval from Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator. Staff will be available to help you relocate and move your materials, if needed.

      Shipping Guidelines

      Shipping to Event: Shipments of exhibit displays, materials or anything pertaining to the exhibit may be shipped no sooner than three (3) days prior to function date if storage space is available. Should your orgnizations packages need to arrive sooner, please contact Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator, (334) 844-4744 or mks@auburn.edu for approval! Storage space is limited. We will do our best to work with you. Shipments larger than 70 lbs. and 108 inches combined will not be accepted unless prior arrangements have been made.

      Please use our Preferred Careers (Fedex and UPS) to ship materials. See the example of how each box or display should be addressed for shipping to the Auburn University Student Center (AUSC).

      **Make sure your organization’s name is on the outside of all materials.**
      One (1) label per box or container shipped
      Materials shipped to AUSC MUST be labeled as follows:

      Auburn University Student Center
      ATTN: Graduate & Professional School Fair- Fall ‘12
      Andrea Conti-Elkins, Student Center Reservations
      255 Heisman Drive
      Auburn, AL 36849-5129

      Hold For: [Insert Organization Name] | [Insert Representative Name]

       

      Make sure the representatives attending the event have ALL tracking numbers for packages shipped to the AUSC prior to the event. We highly recommend you insure ALL of your shipped materials!

      Day of the Event: One the day of the event, beginning at 8:00 am, representatives of your organization may go to the Representative Registration Table in the AUSC 2nd Floor, 2nd Quadrant Lobby to pick-up shipped materials. To pick-up the materials, Auburn University Career Center staff will require a representative's signature.

      Shipping from the Event: Organizations must make their own shipping arrangements, to include billing arrangements with carrier (Preferred Carriers: Fedex and UPS) proper packing and labeling. The AUSC cannot provide billing arrangements. Forms must be filled out completely with the organization's account number.

      ALL boxes must be appropriately labeled and taped! Please take packages to the AUSC 2nd floor, 2nd quadrant lobby where are member of the Auburn University Career Center will be available to log your materials that will be shipped out. The AUSC will make one call to each carrier for pick-up.

      Should your organization choose to use a carrier that is not on the preferred carrier list, your representatives are responsible for arranging pick-up of your materials. Arrange for your carrier to arrive for pick-up by 3:00 pm and no later that 4:00 pm to retrieve the materials.

      Auburn University WILL NOT hold packages awaiting pick up. If the orgnanization's representatives do not attend the event, the organization is responsible for making arrangements for their packages.

      Exhibitor Guidelines
    • Decorations, signs and posters must be coordinated with the Auburn University Student Center (AUSC) staff prior to displaying in the Student Center. No tacks, nails, staples, tape or other fasteners are allowed. Exibitors are liable for any defacement of the facility as a result of their display!
    • The exhibitor is entirely responsible for the space leased by him/her and is responsible for keeping space free from any condiions which would be dangerous to persons coming upon the premises.
    • The management of the AUSC reserves the right to restrict the operation of any exhibit, which in its opinion detracts from the general character of the facility. This includes, but is not limited to, exhibits, which because of noice, flashing light, method of operation or display of unsuitable material are determined by management as objectionable.
    • The Auburn University Career Center and Student Center will not be responsible for any injury that may arise to exhibitors or their employees or for loss of or any damage to any good from any course whatsoever while in transit to or form or while in the AUSC.

    We request your full cooperation in observing these guidelines. Anything not included in the rules and regulations will be subject to the discreion of Auburn University management.
