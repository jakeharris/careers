Sponsorship Opportunities

Career Expo - Star Sponsorship

The Star Sponsorship package is available during registration for the Engineering & Technical Career Expos and the Non-Technical Career Expos held semi-annually in February and September of each year. Star Sponsorship participants receive their choice in booth location (at the career fair they are attending) as well as featured promotion in event flyers, posters and on the Auburn University Career Center website. Star Sponsorship is available for an additional $500 per career expo.
