Part-Time Job Fair



Part-Time Job Fair


 

Wednesday, August 22, 2012
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
Auburn University - Student Center

Event Description

The Part-Time Job Fair is offered annually in the fall and attracts students who are interested in obtaining part-time employment while enrolled at Auburn University. Participating employers can expect to meet students of all class levels and majors. This event is open to employers with both on-campus and off-campus positions.
