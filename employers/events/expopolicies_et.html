Cancellation & No Show Policy

Cancellation Deadline: Friday, August 17, 2012
One half of the registration fee ($300.00) is charged for all cancellations prior to this deadline date whether the registration fee is paid in advance or not. After this deadline, all registration fees MUST be paid in full.

Cancellation Process: In the event you must cancel, please complete the following steps immediately

  • Please call the AU Career Center as soon as possible at (334) 844-3862 and speak with Diane Thorne, Special Events Registration Manager
  • An e-mail must also be sent to Diane Thorne at thornde@auburn.edu explaining that you are officially canceling your organization's participation in the event
  • Diane will send you an e-mail confirming your cancellation. It is important to keep a copy of this communication as documentation for future reference

No Show: If your organization is registered for the event and fails to show up at the event, your organization is expected to pay the balance due.

Failure to pay event fees will cause us to block your organization from utilizing any services available through our office until the matter is cleared. Registration fees cannot be applied to future events.

Please understand that once you have registered and received a confirmation, you MUST abide by the CANCELLATION POLICY. There are NO exceptions!! Any questions or concerns should be directed to:
Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator, (334) 844-4744 or mks@auburn.edu.

We appreciate your continuing cooperation and support of our programs!

Set-up & Equipment Information

Set-up: One 3x6 foot table and one chair will be provided per organization. Ceiling height capacity is 10 feet. Your display needs to fit within the space provided!

Electricity: Electricity will be available, but please bring your own extension cords and power strips.

Attending Both Events? If you are attending both Engineering and Technical and Non-Technical events, your display must be put up and taken down on the day of each event!

Display Guidelines

Space is limited. No additional space can be provided.

Do not bring large displays, furniture or equipment.

Do not rearrange your table, occupy an adjacent vacant table or move to another location without approval from Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator. Staff will be available to help you relocate and move your materials, if needed.

Shipping Guidelines

Shipping to Event: Shipments of exhibit displays, materials or anything pertaining to the exhibit may be shipped no sooner than three (3) days prior to function date. Accommodations for packages needing to arrive earlier must contact Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator, (334) 844-4744 or mks@auburn.edu for approval! Storage space is very limited. We will do our
very best to work with you!

Please use our Preferred Carriers (Fedex and UPS) to ship materials! See the example of how each box or display should be addressed for shipping to The Hotel at Auburn University and Dixon Conference Center (HAUDCC). Your organization's name is required on the outside of all materials. Print off multiple pre-made labels) for your convenience (one (1) per box or container to be shipped).

Make sure the representative(s) attending the event have ALL tracking numbers of packages that have been shipped to HAUDCC prior to arriving at the event. We highly recommend you insure ALL of your materials shipped!

On the day of the event, beginning at 10:00 am, representative(s) of your organization may go to the Auditorium of HAUDCC to pick-up your shipped materials. To receive the items from
an Auburn University Career Center staff member, a signature will be required from your
representative(s).


Shipping from the Event: Patrons must make their own shipping arrangements, to include billing arrangements with the Preferred Carriers (Fedex and UPS). HAUDCC cannot provide billing arrangements, proper packing and labeling. The form must be filled out completely with the organization's account number!

After the Career Expo: ALL boxes must be appropriately labeled and taped! Please take packages to the Auditorium of HAUDCC where a member of the Auburn University Career Center will be available to check-in your materials for shipping out. HAUDCC will make one call to each preferred carrier for pick-up!

Carriers used other than the Preferred Carriers (Fedex and UPS) should arrive by 7:00 pm no later than 8:00 pm to pick-up materials in the Auditorium of HAUDCC the night of the event or between 8:00 am to 12:00 pm, the following day. The representative(s) attending the event for the organization are responsible for making sure the connection is made.

Auburn University will not hold packages awaiting pick-up. If the organization's representative(s) do not attend the event, the organization is responsible for making arrangements to get their packages.
