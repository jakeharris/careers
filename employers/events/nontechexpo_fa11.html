Event Description

The Non-Technical Career Expo is offered semi-annually in the fall and spring semesters and attracts students interested in networking with employers and obtaining full-time employment or internships. Participating employers can expect to meet students in non-technical majors/disciplines and those interested in careers without a scientific or technical focus.
