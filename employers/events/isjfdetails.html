Internship and Summer Job Fair Details


    Additional Information

 

Event Schedule

Please arrive on time and be ready to see students at the start of the event (10:00 AM). If not present by 10:30 AM, you will be considered a “NO SHOW”. Your space can then be assigned to another organization.

8:00 AM-10:00 AM Representative Continental Breakfast
9:00 AM-10:00 AM Representative Registration and Space Setup
10:00 AM-1:00 PM Internship and Summer Job Fair open to Students
1:00 PM Internship and Summer Job Fair Ends

Please plan to stay until 1:00 PM. The time has been publicized and students expect company representatives to be available up until that time.

Registration Fee

Registration Fee: $300 per table. The total registration fee must be paid in full by Friday, January 6, 2012 to keep your reservation.

Registration fee includes:

  • 1 table (3' x 6'), 2 chairs
  • 2 representatives
  • Lunch for up to 2 representatives
  • Parking
  • Electricity (please bring your own extension cords and power strips)
  • Organization information printed in event flyer

Extra Tables: If more than two representatives will be attending, please reserve an additional table for $300.00.
