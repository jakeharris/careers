Parking

Stadium Parking Deck

Space atop the Stadium Parking Deck has been reserved for representatives to park in. It is on a first come first serve basis. It is fairly limited! Upon your arrival, please remember to place your parking pass atop your dashboard in a manner visible from the outside of the vehicle.

A parking pass will be emailed one week prior to the event, and is also available by printing the last page of the tip sheet.

Event Location: Student Center

The Student Center is the location of the event. Its proximity to the Stadium Parking Deck is just another reason why you'll want to arrive early.

Note: there is no parking at the Student Center.

Questions? Ask Parking Services

The Auburn University Parking Services office is located in the South Quad Parking Deck. If you have any questions or concerns concerning your ability to park, don't hesitate to call them at (334) 844 4143 or visit the office directly.
