Part-Time Job Fair Directions & Parking


Printable Tip Sheet


 

Part-Time Job Fair


 



 

 


 

Directions

When making travel arrangements through Atlanta or Columbus, GA please remember that Auburn University is on Central Time and Georgia is on Eastern Time.

For detailed directions to campus traveling from US 280 or I-85 please see the event tip sheet.

To AU Stadium Parking Deck (traveling on AL-147 (US 29) College St.):

  • Turn west off of College St onto Samford Ave, passing in front of Rouse Life Science Building on the right.
  • Continue traveling on Samford through the 4-way stop (Mell St) until you reach the first traffic light.
  • Turn right at the first traffic light onto Duncan Dr. (Telfair Peet Theatre will be on your right)
  • Continue on Duncan Dr., about 250 yds, and you will reach the parking deck at the end of the road on the left.

**If you happen to miss the right turn onto Duncan Dr, continue to the next traffic light at Donahue Dr. Take a right onto Donahue Dr, followed by another right at the next traffic light onto Heisman Dr. Once on Heisman Dr, the Stadium Parking Deck will be approximately 150 yards on the right.

Parking

Arrive EARLY because parking spaces fill quickly!

Parking for representatives is available at the AU Stadium Parking Deck located at the end of Duncan Drive. Upon your arrival, please inform the parking security attendant that you are participating in the Part-Time Job Fair. The is the ONLY place to park on-campus without a parking permit. The Parking Services Department has agreed not to ticket the cars in the lot the entire day ofthe event. Make sure you parking between two (2) white lines.

Guest parking permits can be obtained for other campus lots through the AU Parking Services Department. Parking Services is open between 7:15 AM and 4:15 PM, Monday-Friday and can be contacted at 334-844-4143. Their office is located in the South Quad Parking Deck on the corner of Duncan Dr and Lem Morrison Dr. Our best recommendation is to take advantage of the parking we have available in the Stadium Parking Deck.

You must follow all parking rules and regulations set forth by the Parking Services Department of Auburn University. The Auburn University Career Center will not be responsible for tickets that are received while parked on campus, or wheel-locks and towing fees as a result of parking in a private lot.
