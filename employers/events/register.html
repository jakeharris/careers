Register for a Career Event

(1) Review the detailed information, policies, and guidelines provided for each career events hosted by the Auburn University Career Center. If you have any questions regarding the information you read, please contact Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator, at 334-844-4744 prior to registering.

(2) To register, please use your Tiger Recruiting Link (TRL) account. If you do not have a TRL account, you may create a new one at hire.auburn.edu. Once you have logged in, click on the Events tab in the navy bar across the home screen.

(3) Find the event you wish to register for and click on Register. Complete the registration form and submit.
