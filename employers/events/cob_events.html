College of Business OPCD Events

In an effort to assist employers with their annual recruiting needs, the OPCD coordinates a variety of major specific expo events during both Fall and Spring semesters. Attend the event geared toward your industry to target the specific majors your are looking to hire. For more information visit the Office of Professional and Career Development.
