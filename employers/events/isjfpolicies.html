Internship and Summer Job Fair Policies


    Additional Information

 

Cancellation & No Show Policy

Cancellation Deadline: Friday, January 6, 2012
One half of the registration fee ($150.00) is charged for all cancellations prior to this deadline date whether the registration fee is paid in advance or not. After this deadline, all registration fees MUST be paid in full.

Cancellation Process: In the event you must cancel, please complete the following steps immediately

  • Please call the Auburn University Career Center as soon as possible at (334) 844-3862 and speak with Diane Thorne, Special Events Registration Manager
  • An e-mail must also be sent to Diane Thorne at thornde@auburn.edu explaining that you are officially canceling your organization's participation in the event
  • Diane will send you an e-mail confirming your cancellation. It is important to keep a copy of this communication as documentation for future reference

No Show: If your organization is registered for the event and fails to show up at the event, your organization is expected to pay the balance due.

Failure to pay event fees will cause us to block your organization from utilizing any services available through our office until the matter is cleared. Registration fees cannot be applied to future events.

Please understand that once you have registered and received a confirmation, you MUST abide by the CANCELLATION POLICY. There are NO exceptions!! Any questions or concerns should be directed to:
Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator, (334) 844-4744 or mks@auburn.edu.

We appreciate your continuing cooperation and support of our programs!

Set-up & Equipment Information

Set-up: One 3x6 foot table and one chair (per representative) will be provided. Ceiling height capacity is 10 feet. Your display needs to fit within the space provided! Additional tables (if available) must be reserved prior to the event for a $300 fee.

Electricity: Electricity will be available, but please bring your own extension cords and power strips.

Display Guidelines: Space is limited. Do not bring huge displays, furniture, or equipment. Do not rearrange your table, occupy an adjacent vacant table or move to another location without approval from the Special Events Coordinator.

Shipping Guidelines

Shipping to Event: Shipments of exhibit displays, materials or anything pertaining to the exhibit may be shipped no sooner than ten (10) days prior to function date if storage space is available. Arrangements must be made in advance with Auburn University Career Center personnel. Shipments larger than 70 lbs. and 108 inches combined will not be accepted unless prior arrangements have been made.

**Make sure your organization’s name is on the outside of all materials.**
Materials shipped to the Auburn University Student Center MUST be labeled as follows:

Auburn University Student Center
ATTN: Internship & Summer Job Fair- Spring '12
Hold for
Melvin K. Smith, Special Events Coordinator
Auburn University Career Center
255 Heisman Drive
Auburn, AL 36849-5129


Shipping from the Event: Employers must make their own shipping arrangements, to include billing arrangements with carrier (The Auburn University Career Center and the Auburn University Student Center) cannot provide billing arrangements), proper packing and labeling.
