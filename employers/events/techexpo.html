Event Description

The Engineering & Technical Career Expo is offered semi-annually in the fall and spring semesters and attracts students interested in networking with employers and obtaining full-time employment and internships. Participating employers can expect to meet students in engineering, science, or technical majors/disciplines and those interested in careers in a technical field.
