OCR Information

Employer selection deadline dates may be extended at employer’s request. View each job’s specific dates in TRL on the Position Information page (for students) or the Schedule Details page (for employers). Note: Pre-select (and Pre-select to Alternate) schedules require a ten (10) day lead time, at the least, from job posting date to interview date.

Alternate sign up dates are only applicable to schedules where employers have chosen the "Pre-select to Alternate" model.

Schedule close dates indicate the last day any changes can be made by students or employers, including changing interview times.

All deadlines are at 12:00 midnight of the date listed unless otherwise noted in TRL.
All start dates begin at 12:15 a.m. of the date listed unless otherwise listed in TRL.
