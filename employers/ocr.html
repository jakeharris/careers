On-Campus Recruiting

All offices use Tiger Recruiting Link to manage employer and student interaction. This centralized system is the only place you need post positions, request information sessions, register for events, and schedule on-campus interviews.

For more information on our recruiting services, read below.

On-Campus Interviewing

On-campus interviewing (OCI) offers employers the opportunity to screen candidates for further consideration for positions within the organization. Employers may choose from several different recruiting models that offer varying degrees of control over who you will interview during your visit to Auburn.

Changing or Cancelling an Interviewing Schedule

Please contact the appropriate representative to make changes to your schedule. It is strongly recommended that all changes be made BEFORE deadlines in the schedule occur. If you need to cancel, please contact the appropriate recruiting contact during office hours (7:45AM CT - 4:45PM CT).

Information Sessions

During these sessions, employers make presentations on topics that include the organization's career opportunities, culture, and products or services. The Career Center or the Office of Professional and Career Development will publicize these sessions to the appropriate audience based on the information provided in the Information Session request which can be filed through Tiger Recruiting Link.

Professional or "business casual" dress is appropriate, unless otherwise indicated in the publicity.
