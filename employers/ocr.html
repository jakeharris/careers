On-Campus Recruiting

On-Campus Recruiting (OCR) offers employers the opportunity to identify, screen and interview upcoming graduates for positions within their organizations. Meet face to face with applicants in a professional environment and screen for second round site interviews. Utilize 11 private, comfortably furnished rooms with wireless internet access.

The Auburn University Career Center uses an employer pre-screening system for scheduling campus interviews. This system allows employers to select candidates to interview from resumes submitted by interested students. If you prefer not to pre-screen resumes, interviews will be scheduled through open sign up which allows qualified students to sign-up for your interviews on a first-come, first-served basis.
