Planning Your Visit


Auburn University is located in Auburn, Alabama approximately 55 miles northeast of Montgomery, AL; 100 miles southwest of Atlanta Hartsfield International Airport; and 130 miles southeast of Birmingham, AL in the east-central part of the state.

PLEASE NOTE: Auburn, Alabama is located in the Central Time Zone. Please keep this in mind when making travel arrangements.

Directions to Auburn


To Auburn From Montgomery on I-85 North:
  • Get off I-85 N at EXIT 51
  • Take a LEFT at the light onto S. College Street.
  • Stay on College Street for about 4 miles. You will arrive at Thach Avenue and will see Samford Hall on the left hand side of the street and the University Chapel on the right.
To Auburn From Atlanta on I-85 South:
  • Get off I-85 S at EXIT 57. Follow road to the right.
  • At the second traffic light, take a LEFT onto Glenn Avenue.
  • Stay on Glenn Ave. for about 4 miles
  • After you turn onto Glenn Avenue you will travel through six traffic lights. The seventh light will be College Street.
  • Take a LEFT onto College Street
  • Travel to the next traffic light, Magnolia Avenue (you are now at Toomer's Corner)
  • As you cross the intersection, J&M Bookstore will be on the left side of the road, and Auburn University will be on the right.
To Auburn from Birmingham on Highway US-280E:
  • Take a RIGHT off US-280E onto AL-147/College Street (there will be a Conoco Service Station at the intersection)
  • Stay on AL-147 for 4.8 miles. It is a straight shot to Auburn where AL-147 will turn into College Street.
  • After you cross the railroad tracks you will enter downtown Auburn.
  • The light following the tracks will be at Glenn Ave. Go through the intersection.
  • The next intersection will be Magnolia Avenue (Toomer's Corner)
  • As you cross the intersection, J&M Bookstore will be on the left side of the road, and Auburn University will be on the right.

Parking and Directions to Martin Hall


Parking on Campus: On most college campuses, parking is very limited. This is especially true at Auburn around Martin Hall. We will issue you a campus Guest Parking Pass if you would contact us prior to your visit. Parking passes can be picked up in 400 Martin Hall on the morning of your visit. You are encouraged to park in one of two 15 minute parking spaces directly across from the front entrance of the building. The meter spaces across the street from Martin Hall are for guests with a guest permit. You must put money in these meters if you park in those spaces. If you park on campus, please observe all parking rules and regulations set forth by the University Police Department. Career Services will not be responsible for tickets received while parked on campus.

Restaurants in Auburn


There are many fine restaurants in the downtown Auburn area that are convenient to Mary Martin Hall and Lowder Business Building. There are also several restaurants that will deliver your food. There is a notebook of restaurants and their menus available at the fourth floor desk (in Martin Hall).
