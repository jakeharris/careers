Student Employment

The mission of the Auburn University Student Employment Program in the Auburn University Career Center is to provide part-time employment opportunities to currently enrolled Auburn University students. Student employment fosters leadership and career development, substantive employment opportunities, transferable life skills, and personal growth for student employees.

Tiger Recruiting Link (TRL)

Please post ALL student employment positions (on-campus and off-campus) in Tiger Recruiting Link (TRL) for a minimum of three (3) business days. The Auburn University Career Center directs students to this site to locate part-time jobs, and we work hard to ensure equal access to jobs for all students. In using TRL, you can post your job announcements at no charge for up to 180 days at a time.
