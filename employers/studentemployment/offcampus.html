Off Campus Employers

Posting a Position

Please post ALL student employment positions (on-campus and off-campus) in Tiger Recruiting Link (TRL) for a minimum of three (3) business days. The Auburn University Career Center directs students to this site to locate part-time jobs, and to ensure compliance with equal opportunity laws, we ask that you post your positions on TRL for many students to view.

Recognizing Top Student Employees

The Career Center encourages Auburn Univeristy departments, colleges and offices as well as Auburn/Opelika area businesses to celebrate student employee contributions during National Student Employment Week. NSEW enables us to thank Auburn students for the contributions they make as employees to the University and the surrounding community.

  • Student Employee of the Year Nominations (Accepted beginning January 2013)
