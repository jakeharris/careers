Celebrate Your Student Employees

Suggestions for Celebrations

  • Host a special gathering with staff and student employees. Ideas include: pizza party, potluck, sundae bar, cookies/cake, sub sandwiches, etc.
  • Write a thank you note for a job well done
  • Design and present your student employees with certificates of appreciation
  • Post a sign or banner in your office- "We Appreciate Our Student Workers"
  • Nominate an outstanding student employee for Student Employee of the Year
  • Prepare "care packages" to give student employess with popcorn, trail mix, candy, pens, sticky notes, etc.

Send Us Your Pictures

Send pictures of your student employee celebrations and you might be featured on the Career Center website! Email pictures to Meaghan Weir at meaghan@auburn.edu.
