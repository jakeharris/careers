National Student Employment Week- April 9- April 15, 2012

Each year Auburn University celebrates National Student Employment Week (NSEW), during the second full week of April. While student employment offers students career enhancing opportunities, they in turn make substantial contributions to the operation of the University and the local community. The Career Center encourages Auburn Univeristy departments, colleges and offices as well as Auburn/Opelika area businesses to celebrate student employee contributions during National Student Employment Week. NSEW enables us to thank Auburn students for the contributions they make as employees to the University and the surrounding community.

Student Employee of the Year

During National Student Employment Week festivities, the Student Employee of the Year Recognition Ceremony will honor two outstanding student employees selected from nominated candidates, one employed on-campus and one employed in the community.

Eligibility
  • Must be a current Auburn University student in good academic standing enrolled for the Spring 2012 semester.
  • Must have worked three (3) months full-time or six (6) months part-time between July 1, 2011 and May 31, 2012 (anticipated). This is equivalent to 240 working hours during the year period.
  • May include undergraduate or graduate students who are on student employment payroll (this does include Resident Assistants).
  • Graduate, Research, or Teaching Assistants are NOT eligible.
Nominations

Only complete nominations will be considered. Complete nominations include the following:

  • 2012 Nomination Form (Available online January 2012)
  • Letter of Nomination - 2 pg limit (Only ONE letter will be considered for each nominee)
Selection Criteria

An independent selection committee comprised of University staff members and community employers will rate the following qualities for each nominee:

  • Reliability
  • Initiative
  • Quality of Work
  • Professionalism
  • Uniqueness of Contribution
Process

Two (2) winners will be chosen by the selection committee; one employed on-campus and one employed in the Auburn/Opelika community. The Career Center will submit the on-campus winner's nomination letter to compete in the regional competition (SASEA). The regional winner will receive a $1000 money order and will compete for the title of national Student Employee of the Year (NSEA).

     

Support Opportunities

National Student Employment Week (NSEW) presents a unique and rare opportunity for your business to have an on-campus marketing presence. All support will be recognized on the event website and in all marketing materials. You can be a sponsor in one of the following ways:

  • Offering a discount or special promotion during the month of April to Auburn University student employees who present a pay stub and a Auburn student ID. Examples: $5 off a meal, free pastry with purchase of coffee drink, 10% off any purchase, reduced admission prices, buy one get one free, etc.
  • Provide an item for the Student Employee of the Year winners' (qty: 2) giftbags. Suggested items include: gift cards, gift baskets, t-shirts, tickets, professional portfolios, etc.
  • Provide promotional items to be handed out to students during the week or to be placed in Student Employee of the Year nominees' goodie bags (approx: 25-40). Suggestions items include: pens, cups, coozies, t-shirts, coupons, etc.

Thank you to those who support this recognition program! For more information please visit the Supporter Information Page or contact Meaghan Weir at 334-844-3867.
