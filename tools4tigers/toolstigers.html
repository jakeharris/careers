REGISTRATION
7:30 AM – 8:00 AM  
8:00 AM – 8:05 AM

 Welcome
LAY YOUR FOUNDATION
8:05 AM – 9:00 AM Draw Your Blueprints: Welcome & Goal Setting
Learn how to establish short and long-term goals to equip yourself for planning an effective job search.
9:00 AM – 10:00 AM Measure Your Strengths: StrengthsQuest Interpretation
This instrument assesses and identifies the top strengths that you possess and can offer to employers. You will understand your results and how your strengths compliment other strengths and team environments. You will receive emailed directions so you may complete the StrengthsQuest instrument no later than Friday, December 9.
10:00 AM – 10:15 AM Break/Light Snack/Drinks
10:15 AM – 11:15 AM Find Your Personality Balance: TypeFocus Interpretation
This instrument identifies your personality type. Through the session you will understand how to highlight your personality to an employer and learn in what work environments you might naturally excel and which ones you might find a challenge. Go to http://www.typefocus.com/ and use "au7788" as your Site Password. Complete the personality assessment no later than Friday, December 9.
11:30 PM – 1:30 PM

 Business & Etiquette Lunch
PACK YOUR TOOLBOX
1:45 PM – 2:45 PM Drill into Your Network: Personal Brand / On-Line Job Search / Networking Strategies
Learn how to network and search for jobs using up-to-date resources like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, CareerShift and Tiger Recruiting Link, jobs.auburn.edu.
2:45 PM – 3:00 PM

 Light Snack/Drinks
3:00 PM – 4:00 PM Hammer out the Details: Resume, Cover Letter Writing, Reading Job Descriptions
Learn how to effectively read a job description and write a resume and cover letter to get the job.
4:00 PM – 5:00 PM Nail the Interview: Interview Strategies
Learn how to effectively prepare and navigate the interview as well as weigh pros and cons of various interview question answers.
