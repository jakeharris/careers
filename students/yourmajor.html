By attending this workshop, you will learn how to:

  • Navigate the career/major exploration process
  • Enhance self-awareness through assessments including the Strong Interest Inventory and TypeFocus
  • Explore career options and specific majors available at Auburn University
  • Evaluate career options based on professional interests, personality, values

Session Outline

Getting Started - Overview of the career decision making process and the colleges/schools at Auburn under which the majors are housed.

Who Are You? - Self-Assessment
Strong Interest Inventory interpretation - Assess your professional interests through the Strong Interest Inventory. This interpretation will review the student's assessment results and will help the student understand how their professional interests can lead to careers.

TypeFocus interpretation - Assess your personality through the TypeFocus. This interpretation will review the student's personality type and will help the student understand the role personality plays in evaluating careers and majors.

Putting the Pieces Together - Learn how professional interests, personality and values work together to help students evaulate major and career options and make more informed decisions.

Explore Auburn Majors - Students will meet with representatives from 3 of the colleges/schools at Auburn to learn more about major options available.

Next Steps - Career decision making is a process and will take time. Learn the recommend next steps to take following this workshop.
