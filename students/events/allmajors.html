All Majors Career Expo Spring 2013


Tuesday, March 26
3:00 PM - 8:00 PM
The Hotel at AU and Dixon Conference Center

The All Majors Career Expo will host companies who will be looking to hire students in the following schools/colleges: business, accounting, banking, government, human services, retail, sales, communication, liberal arts, and international relations.

No matter your major and no matter your degree level, there are opportunities for you. Don't miss this opportunity to meet with many employers in one location.
