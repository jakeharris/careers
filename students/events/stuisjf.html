Internship and Summer Job Fair Spring 2013

Wednesday, January 16
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
The Student Center



Internship and Summer Job Fair allows students to meet with representatives and view exhibits from camps, theme parks, businesses, and organizations who are seeking students as interns, summer workers, or trainees in all majors.

E-Resume Review

Attach your Word resume to an email. Your resume will be reviewed and sent back to you with suggested revisions within 48 hours.

Send your resume to eresume@auburn.edu with Resume in the subject line.

To have your resume reviewed by someone in the Career Center before the Internship and Summer Job Fair, send it as an email attachment to eresume@auburn.edu with ISJF Resume in the subject line.

There is a 48 hour turn around.
