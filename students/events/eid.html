Education Interview Day Fall 2013

Tuesday, October 15
8:00 AM - 3:00 PM
The Hotel at AU and Dixon Conference Center



Recruiters and representatives from school districts throughout Alabama and the Southeast attend Education Interview Day. Education majors, Health and Human Performance (PE program) majors, Pro-Teach students and others seeking full-time positions should attend.

Business Professional Dress is required.
